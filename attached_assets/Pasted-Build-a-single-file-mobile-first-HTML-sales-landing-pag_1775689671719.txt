Build a single-file mobile-first HTML sales landing page (no frameworks, pure HTML/CSS/JS) 
for a Google Business Profile optimization service targeting small Brazilian business owners. 
The page must work perfectly on mobile screens (375px–430px wide) and feel like a premium, 
immersive experience — NOT a generic template.

---

## CONTEXT & GOAL

The reader is a small business owner (bakery, market, salon, pet shop, etc.) with a physical 
location in a small Brazilian city. They have little digital knowledge and don't yet understand 
the power of Google Business Profile. The page must EDUCATE them first, then SELL. 

The final CTA is to generate a PIX payment of R$175,00 (entry installment of a R$350 service).

---

## AESTHETIC DIRECTION

- Dark background (#0A0A0F) with deep green neon accents (#00FF88) and warm amber highlights 
  (#FFB800) — professional, modern, trustworthy but with energy
- Use Google Fonts: "Syne" (700/800) for headlines + "DM Sans" (400/500) for body text
- Cinematic scroll experience: each section animates in as the user scrolls (IntersectionObserver)
- Subtle grain texture overlay on background (CSS noise filter or SVG feTurbulence)
- Glassmorphism cards (backdrop-filter: blur) for stat blocks and service details
- Smooth scroll behavior throughout
- No tacky stock photo vibes — use emoji and CSS illustrations instead of images

## PAGE STRUCTURE (scroll narrative — the user is "walked through" a story)

### SECTION 1 — HERO (full viewport height)
- Animated headline that types itself out: "Seu negócio aparece no Google quando alguém 
  procura o que você vende?"
- Subheadline: "Se a resposta for não — ou você não souber — esse serviço é pra você."
- Floating badge: "📍 Atendimento em [cidade]"
- Pulsing scroll-down arrow
- Background: animated radial gradient that breathes slowly

### SECTION 2 — PAIN POINT ("A realidade brutal")
- Title: "Enquanto você está trabalhando duro, seus concorrentes aparecem primeiro."
- 3 scenario cards, each representing a typical business owner situation:
  Card 1: "🔍 Cliente pesquisa 'açougue perto de mim' — você não aparece"
  Card 2: "📸 Seu perfil tem 2 fotos borradas tiradas em 2019"
  Card 3: "⭐ Zero avaliações — cliente escolhe o concorrente com 47 reviews"
- Each card slides in from the left with a 200ms stagger
- Design: red-tinted glassmorphism cards to reinforce pain

### SECTION 3 — EDUCATION ("Por que o Google Business muda tudo")
- Title: "O Google Business Profile é o seu vendedor digital. Gratuito. 24h por dia."
- Animated counter stats (count up on scroll enter):
  → 97% das pessoas pesquisam no Google antes de visitar um negócio
  → 42% mais pedidos de rota com fotos profissionais
  → 5x mais chamadas para negócios com perfil otimizado
- Visual: simple animated diagram showing "Pesquisa Google" → "Perfil otimizado" → "Cliente 
  na porta" with connecting arrows that draw themselves

### SECTION 4 — THE SERVICE ("O que você recebe")
- Title: "Tudo feito por você. Você só abre a porta."
- Vertical checklist with green checkmarks animating in one by one:
  ✅ Criação ou otimização completa do seu perfil Google
  ✅ Categoria certa para aparecer nas buscas do seu segmento
  ✅ Descrição profissional do negócio (persuasiva, não genérica)
  ✅ 15 fotos profissionais tiradas no seu estabelecimento
  ✅ Horários, endereço, telefone e site 100% corretos
  ✅ Configuração para aparecer no Google Maps
  ✅ Entrega em até 2 semanas
- Emphasis block: "Você não precisa saber nada de tecnologia. Nós cuidamos de tudo."

### SECTION 5 — PROOF / SOCIAL PROOF ("Resultado real")

  green glow — like showing a real phone screen

### SECTION 6 — INVESTMENT ("O investimento")
- Title: "Menos que um anúncio no jornal. Resultado que dura meses."
- Large price display:
  R$ 350,00

## TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

- Single HTML file with embedded CSS and JS — no external dependencies except Google Fonts 
  (loaded via @import in CSS)
- IntersectionObserver API for all scroll-triggered animations
- Smooth counting animation for stats (requestAnimationFrame)
- Accordion FAQ with CSS transitions only
- Clipboard API for PIX key copy
- All animations must respect prefers-reduced-motion
- Page must load fast — no heavy assets, everything CSS-based
- Meta tags: viewport, og:title, og:description for WhatsApp link preview
- The page should have a `<title>` and favicon emoji: 📍
